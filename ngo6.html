About Us

Parisar Foundation is dedicated to advocating for sustainable urban living and creating safer, more inclusive cities. Its mission is to foster environmental consciousness, improve mobility, and ensure healthier urban ecosystems.

Causes Supported:

• Urban environmental conservation • Sustainable transportation advocacy • Air quality improvement • Road safety and public space management • Environmental education and awareness

Year of Establishment: 1982
