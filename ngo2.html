About Us

The IDEA Foundation is committed to empowering marginalized communities through education, skill development, and social awareness. Its vision is to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to achieve their full potential.


Causes Supported:

• Education for underprivileged children • Women empowerment through skill training • Community development • Health and hygiene awareness


Year of Establishment: 2002
