About Us

The Akshar Paaul Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through education and skill development. The foundation envisions a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and opportunities to achieve their potential

Causes Supported:

• Education for underprivileged children • Skill development and vocational training • Women empowerment through literacy and skills • Community-based awareness programs

Year of Establishment: 2015
