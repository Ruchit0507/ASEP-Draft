About Us

Navkshitij is dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive environment for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Its mission is to provide them with opportunities to lead fulfilling lives through education, skill development, and meaningful engagement. The vision of Navkshitij is to build a society that respects and celebrates diversity.

Causes Supported:

• Care and rehabilitation for individuals with intellectual disabilities

• Life skills training for self-reliance

• Promoting social inclusion

• Educational and therapeutic programs

Year of Establishment: 2003
