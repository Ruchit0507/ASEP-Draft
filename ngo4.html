About Us

Majha Ghar Foundation is committed to providing shelter and support to underprivileged individuals and families. Its vision is to ensure that every person has a safe and dignified place to call home, fostering stability and opportunities for growth.

Causes Supported:

•Affordable housing for the needy • Rehabilitation of homeless families • Support services for underprivileged communities • Livelihood programs for sustainable living

Year of Establishment: 2010
