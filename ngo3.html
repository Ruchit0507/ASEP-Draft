About Us

The Applied Environmental Research Foundation (AERF) is dedicated to conserving biodiversity, promoting sustainable development, and empowering communities to adopt eco-friendly practices. Its vision is to bridge the gap between research and actionable conservation efforts.


Causes Supported:

• Biodiversity conservation • Climate change mitigation • Community-driven sustainable development • Environmental education and awareness


Year of Establishment: 1995
